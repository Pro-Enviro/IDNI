Industrial Decarbonisation Northen Ireland Project

Industrial Decarbonisation Northern Ireland (ID-NI) is an ambitious project led by InvestNI to bring together key stakeholders from across Northern Ireland to develop a plan for industrial decarbonisation.

Northern Ireland has unique opportunities and challenges when tackling industrial decarbonisation, including the opportunity to become a world-leading industrial cluster for decarbonised manufacturing

Three partners from the Black Country Industrial Cluster (Camirus, Pro Enviro and The EIU) are working with InvestNI, Queens University Belfast, Ulster University, manufacturing NI and MPANI and all the local authorities to develop a vision for industrial decarbonisation that also enhances productivity and competitiveness for investors.

Our Vision

The project likely aims to demonstrate clear results in terms of carbon reduction and increased productivity, contributing to the broader goals of sustainability and economic development.

Our Mission

The primary focus on reducing emissions indicates a commitment to addressing climate change and environmental impact. This value aligns with sustainability goals and signifies a dedication to reduce the carbon footprint of industries in Northern Ireland.
