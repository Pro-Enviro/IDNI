Average Daily Consumption, kWh per Half-Hour Chart Guide

This chart presents the average half-hourly consumption profile per day of the week. The horizontal axis of the chart represents the time of day. The vertical axis shows the scale of electricity consumption in kWh.
Viewers can observe key features such as peak consumption times, differences in consumption patterns between weekdays and weekends, and any other notable trends or variations.
