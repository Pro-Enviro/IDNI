Maximum Demand Chart Guide

The data points of the maximum demand chart are simply calculated from the maximum kWh used in a single half-hourly period for each day of the period, then multiplied by 2 to get the maximum demand in a half hour period in kW. The chart therefore shows the highest load that occurred each day on a half-hourly basis.
