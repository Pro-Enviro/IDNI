ASHP

Air Source Heat Pumps (ASHPs) absorb heat from the outside air in the same way that a fridge extracts heat from within. Air source heat pumps can extract heat from the air when outside temperatures are as low as minus 15°C

Infrared Heating

This wavelength of the electromagnetic spectrum provides a large but gentle spread of heat known as ‘comfort heat’ and works like the Sun’s non-visible part of the spectrum. Far-infrared heaters require less power to create heat and typically run at 98.5% efficiency, with a quicker heat-up period compared to near-infrared heaters.

heat_pump_balance

Convection Heaters

Electric convection heaters with timer and temperature controls provide heating in the workplace to optimise efficiency. These units typically have an efficiency rating of <98%.

hvac

Storage Heaters

Storage heaters are more advanced versions of traditional electric storage heaters and are able to store heat for much longer and release it in a more controlled way.

mode_heat_cool

Stratified Warm Air

The production areas contain high apex ceilings. Such areas suffer from stratification of warm air that rises to the apex of the ceiling, which then is eventually lost to the atmosphere. This phenomenon increases the load factor of the associated heating system as it tackles the heat loss from the operative level.

toys_fan

De-Stratification Fans

Warm air rises in buildings with high ceilings, and heat is lost through the roof. De-stratification fans blow warm air back to ground level where it is needed and can save up to 20% on typical energy bills.

emergency_heat_2

Heating Controls

The heating system is controlled via temperature and time controls.Additionally, the radiators have TRVs (Thermostatic Radiator Valves) which increase the resolution of control. Due to this efficient level of control, the system only operates when required, reducing wasted heat generation.

nest_thermostat

TRVs

TRVs can be installed on each of the radiators to enable an increased resolution of control and, therefore, reduce the load factor on the boiler. Smart TRVs that are wirelessly connected to a control hub can be installed, enabling the building manager to remotely reduce the TRVs in each room.

mode_dual

Free Cooling

Typically, you can expect a free cooling system to remain in operation while external temperatures are below 12°C. This is an energy-efficient way to maintain cellar temperatures.

fireplace

Immersion Heater

Hot water is provided by an immersion tank, which utilises a 3kW coil heater. The system lacks controls and heats the water continuously.

water_voc

Instantaneous Water Heater

These systems provide hot water on demand rather than unnecessarily storing and heating hot water when not required. Instantaneous water heaters, also known as tankless water heaters or on-demand water heaters, work by heating water as it flows through the unit.
