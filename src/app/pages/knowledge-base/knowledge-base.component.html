Warm air rises in buildings with high ceilings, and heat is lost through the roof. De-stratification fans blow warm air back to ground level where it is needed and can save up to 20% on typical energy bills.

PVC Curtains

PVC strip curtain is installed in any doorways that are used frequently. PVC strip curtains provide an easily movable barrier to the outside that will stop mixing the two airstreams at different temperatures.

Pipe Lagging

The pipes can be insulated with lagging, which can be easily self-installed. This will reduce the heatloss in the storage areas and therefore reduce energy consumption from the boiler.

Heating Controls

The smart thermostat can be installed to ensure that the heating system only operated when necessary, therefore reducing the system's energy consumption.

TRVs

TRVs can be installed on each of the radiators to enable an increased resolution of control and, therefore, reduce the load factor on the boiler.

Free Cooling

Typically, you can expect a free cooling system to remain in operation while external temperatures are below 12°C. This is an energy efficient way to maintain cellar temperatures.

Immersion Heater

These systems provide hot water on demand rather than unnecessarily storing and heating hot water when not required. Instantaneous water heaters, also known as tankless water heaters or on-demand water heaters, work by heating water as it flows through the unit.
